Technical Skills/Experience

I am currently a senior at the California State University of Sacramento and will be graduating with my Bachelors Degree in Computer Science in may. I currently work for the Sustainable Technology Optimization Research Center (STORC) at my university and have provided computer support to multiple projects there a few of which can be viewed in the Frontend and ZNE repositories on GitHub, and manage some of their website content.

The majority of my background is in Java, due to the CSUS curriculum, but through my work at STORC as well as through outside projects I have gained some experience working with Python, C/C++, HTML, CSS, JavaScript and C# as well as the ASP.NET CORE framework. I love to learn and am always curious to new languages and tools to accomplish my goals. I enjoy creating things that are valued by others as well as hold a visual appeal which is why I am most interested in securing a job that involves application developement in a team environment.

Personal Interests

I have a wide range of interests and have been blessed with many opportunities to explore them throughout my life. Below are the things in life I find most fulfilling.

Travel:


I love to travel, and because of my mom's equal wanderlust, I have been to Africa (Ethopia, Tanzania, Morroco), Central America (Guatemala), and Europe (Italy, Spain) for multiple weeks as well as many of the States. The new perspective that comes with firsthand exposure to a different culture is something I think everyone should have the opportunity to experience.

Spanish:
I have an intermediate understanding of the Spanish language and am finishing my fourth year of three and a half years of it last spring. My reading comprehension is greater than my speaking ability which I hope to improve by living and working in a Spanish speaking country at some point in the near future.

Yoga:
I was first introduced to yoga in 2014 by a friend who wanted to try a new method of working out. She quickly realized that it was not for her, however I have been doing it ever since. Originally I enjoyed it because of physical challenge it presented, and later turned to it to help with my anxiety. I stuck with it for two years and it became a very valued part of my life and routine. It's focus on self-love and the peace that comes from weekly meditation is just as important to me as my growing ability to do cool and complex poses. I currently haven't practiced it for several months but plan on returning to it soon.
