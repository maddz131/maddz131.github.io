Technical Skills/Experience

I am currently a junior at the California State University of Sacramento and am working towards earning my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science as well as a certificate in Software Engineering. I work as a developer for the STORC organization of CSUS and create and maintain Flask applications that dynamically displays critical data which is used by the STORC scientists as they grow plants using a hydroponic system.

The majority of my background is in Java, due to the CSUS curriculum, but through my work as well as outside projects I have gained experience in the Python, BASH, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript languages as well as the Django and Flask web frameworks. My real passion lies in creating a product that serves to benefit the environment or people in need and, post-graduation, I hope to join a company whose interests align with mine.
